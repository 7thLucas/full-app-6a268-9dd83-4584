The Problem

Print shop chaos costs you
jobs, margins, and sleep

  • Missed deadlines — jobs tracked on whiteboards and spreadsheets fall through the cracks under pressure.

  • Invisible material costs — you finish a job without knowing if you made money on it.

  • No operational visibility — no single view of what's in progress, overdue, or at risk right now.

The Solution

One dashboard.
Full operational control.

  • Track every job from intake to delivery — status, specs, client, and due date in one place.

  • See material cost per job — log paper, ink, and substrates; know your margin before you invoice.

  • Automatic deadline alerts — overdue and at-risk jobs surface immediately; nothing slips unnoticed.
