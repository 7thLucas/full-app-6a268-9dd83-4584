# PrintDesk — Product Overview

## What It Is
PrintDesk is a web application for printing shop owners to manage print jobs, track material costs, and meet delivery deadlines. North star: deliver quality prints on time while keeping material costs in control.

## Users & Personas
- **Print Shop Owner** — operates the business, monitors costs, tracks profitability, and owns delivery commitments to clients.
- **Shop Manager / Operator** — handles day-to-day job intake, updates job statuses, and logs material consumption.

## Positioning
A focused operations tool for independent and small-to-mid print shops. Not a full ERP or accounting system — a lean, practical dashboard that eliminates the chaos of managing multiple jobs, materials, and deadlines at the same time.

## Core Features

### 1. Job Management
Create and track print jobs end-to-end. Each job records: client name, job type, quantity, print specs (size, material, finish), status (Queued → In Progress → Completed → Delivered), and delivery due date.

### 2. Material Cost Tracking
Log material consumption per job: paper, ink, substrate, and finishing materials. See cost per job and cumulative material spend over any period. Identify which jobs are unprofitable before invoicing.

### 3. Delivery Deadline Tracking
Assign due dates per job. The dashboard flags overdue and at-risk jobs automatically. No job slips without being noticed.

### 4. Operations Dashboard
At-a-glance view of active jobs, overdue deadlines, material spend, and on-time delivery rate. Replaces whiteboards and spreadsheets with a single operational screen.

## Strategic Principles
- **Simplicity first** — shop owners are not software experts; the UI must be immediately legible with zero training.
- **Operational focus** — every feature directly reduces missed deadlines or cost overruns. Nothing decorative.
- **Speed of entry** — adding a job and logging materials must be fast; friction kills adoption.

## Brand & Tone
Professional, calm, practical. Direct language — shop-floor vocabulary, not enterprise jargon. Color identity: deep ink blue as primary, amber as accent.

## Scope
**In scope (MVP):** job management, material cost logging, deadline tracking, operational dashboard.
**Out of scope:** invoicing, customer-facing portals, multi-location inventory, payroll, advanced accounting.
